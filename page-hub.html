Health Sciences

“Welcome to the Health Sciences Division of Cape Fear Community College. On behalf of the Health Sciences Division, we would like to express our excitement that you have chosen to visit our site and we hope that the information you find on this site will be helpful to you.

To address the demand for highly qualified health professionals, we offer a diverse set of associate degree and diploma programs in Nursing and Allied Health. We offer Medical Sonography, Cardiovascular Sonography and Radiography, Pharmacy Technology, Surgical Technology, Phlebotomy, Occupational Therapy Assistant, Dental Hygiene, Dental Assisting, Medical Assisting, Human Services Technology/Substance Abuse, Nurse Aide, Practical Nursing and Associate Degree Nursing, Nursing Transition, and RIBN programs.”

Dr. Angela R. Ballentine, Dean of Health Sciences
